Bountiful Foods Site Plan

Site Purpose

Bountiful Foods is a fresh foods drink creator. They have been around for a very long time (1920) and have a variety of drinks. Bountiful Foods aims to help people find drinks that help energize them and propel them throughout their day. We inspire creativity in your life by allowing you to control what you drink with our custom drink option. We hope to eventually grow and spread through the valley. However, the first step in doing this is building a website to attract a larger market. We hope to expand our marketing efforts and take in a larger market share in the healthy drink industry.

Target Audience

Mothers

Mothers who wish to provide their children with healthy alternatives. Whether they are seeking for fresh ingredients for side dishes in their evening meals or a tasty drink, Bountiful Foods can provide the best and healthiest products. We will Advertise our fruit baskets to appeal to these individuals.

Dieters

These are individuals who are currently on a dieting program or who are seeking to lose weight. We will appeal to these individuals by advartising the organic, healthy, and fresh aspects of the food we are selling.
